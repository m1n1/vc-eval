Any-to-Any Voice Conversion Systems Evaluation

This evaluation is part of my Bachelor Thesis "State-of-the-art any-to-any Voice Conversion". There are 3 different voice conversion systems compared here, One-shot Voice Conversion by Separating Speaker and Content Representations with Instance Normalization (ADAIN) [1] [2], AGAIN-VC: A One-shot Voice Conversion using Activation Guidance and Adaptive Instance Normalization [3] [4] and FragmentVC: Any-to-Any Voice Conversion by End-to-End Extracting and Fusing Fine-Grained Voice Fragments With Attention [5] [6]

All seen speakers are part of CSTR's VCTK Corpus [7]. The utterances for unseen speakers were manually extracted from the Joe Rogan Podcast [8]
